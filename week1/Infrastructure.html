Campus

Watch the video on YouTube

The College is spread over a built-up area of over 36,000 sqm. on a sprawling 33-acre campus amidst verdant surroundings.

Facilities

  • 95 spacious classrooms and 119 labs (all classrooms and labs are equipped with LCD projectors)
  • Air-conditioned conference rooms
  • An independent three storied Library building
  • An independent four storied Student Centre (with canteen, gym, indoor sports, placement cell)
  • Auditorium (seating capacity: 1500, fully air-conditioned, equipped with WiFi and 4 high powered LCD projectors)
  • Extensive outdoor sports facilities
  • Hostel
  • Health Centre
  • All laboratories are supported by UPS and continuous power back-up from captive generators (> 500kVA).

Library

The College has a dedicated three-storied building with a total area of 1460 Sqm, for the exclusive use of Library and Postgraduate Research facilities.

The College library has 87,582 books, 17,046 reference titles and 191 journals that cater to all the eight branches of engineering, as well as other disciplines.

A Digital Library has also been set up with 30 multimedia PCs, with full access to Digital Libraries of IEEE, ASME, ASCE, DELNET, IESTC, KNIMBUS with Remote Access, NDLI and NPTEL.

All major newspapers, news magazines and books of general interest (non-fiction, biographies, rare books etc.) are also made available.

College Bus facility is available up to 6:00 PM on all working days to allow students to spend time in the library.

Labs

The College has well-equipped labs (well beyond prescribed syllabus requirements in some cases). Please refer to individual department pages for more details.

Hostel on Campus

Our belief that a residential campus offers a superior environment for academics turned into a reality during 2009-10. Excellent and facilitative provision of Hostel accommodation is a major support service offered by CVR College of Engineering.

Moving away from home to study is an exciting time in life. There are new friends to be made, new places to discover, new chances to be explored. Every effort has been made at the Hostel to provide the best facilities for students who live on campus. Every student has access to a wide range of activities, facilities and support.

The capacity of the two boys' hostels and two girls' hostels combined is 1250 in the academic year 2025-26.

Student Centre

The Student Centre is an independent four story building which houses various facilities such as canteen, gym, indoor sports facilities, and placement office.

Health Centre

The Health Centre at the College provides essential medical services with a dedicated doctor, a small team of nurses, and basic inpatient facilities, including a few beds for students and staff requiring short-term care. The Centre is equipped with an ambulance to handle emergencies and transport patients to larger hospitals when necessary.
